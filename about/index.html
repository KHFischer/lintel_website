About Lintel

Lintel Standard, Inc. is a Delaware C-Corporation founded in 2025. The company builds cryptographic strategy-verification infrastructure for systematic hedge funds and their allocators. Two published specifications define the system: the Open Veracity Language (OVL) and the Computation Veracity Protocol (CVP).

The platform is independent of the funds it verifies. Auditors are independent of the firms they audit for the same structural reason — the value of the verification depends on the verifier having no stake in the outcome.

OVL is public. Lintel's implementation of it is proprietary. The format is a standard anyone can read, inspect, or build against — in the same relationship that PDF holds to Adobe.

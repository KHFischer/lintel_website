STRATEGY VERIFICATION

Capital allocation runs on trust, not proof.

Lintel produces cryptographic proof that a systematic strategy produced its claimed results. Strategies execute inside sealed hardware. The logic is never transmitted to Lintel or to anyone else.

MECHANISM

How it works

Verification happens in three steps. The strategy is specified in a declarative language, executed inside sealed hardware, and packaged as a tamper-evident proof. No party, including Lintel, needs access to the strategy logic.

Specify

Strategies are written in the Open Veracity Language — a declarative specification of roughly 25 mathematical primitives. OVL is published, inspectable, and implementable by any party.

Seal and execute

The specification is compiled and executed inside a Trusted Execution Environment on commodity confidential-computing hardware. The strategy logic never leaves the sealed environment.

Prove

Execution emits a Strategy Verification Package — a tamper-evident, hardware-attested record that binds claimed results to the computation that produced them.

AUDIENCE

Who uses Lintel

Lintel sits between the party producing a verifiable claim and the parties that depend on that claim being true. Four profiles recur.

FOR MANAGERS

Shorter operational due diligence, larger allocator universe.

Systematic managers raise capital on claimed performance. Lintel converts those claims into cryptographic proofs — verifiable by any allocator, with no disclosure of the strategy that produced them. The result is shorter operational due diligence and access to allocators who require evidence, not attestation letters.

FOR ALLOCATORS

Evidence-based allocation. Faster due diligence cycles.

Operational due diligence currently depends on documents, interviews, and inference. Lintel replaces parts of that with hardware-attested evidence — proof that a strategy produced the returns claimed, without requiring the manager to disclose what the strategy does. The result is evidence-based allocation, earlier detection of model drift, and capacity to evaluate emerging managers who would otherwise be screened out by ODD constraints.

FOR CAPITAL INTRODUCTION

Recommendation quality backed by evidence.

Capital introduction desks stake their careers and fee income on the managers they recommend. Lintel provides a layer of evidence that sits between the manager's deck and the allocator's due diligence — a pre-screen that filters out misrepresented track records before they become a reputational incident. The result is higher-quality recommendations, reduced exposure to fraud risk, and a credible path to introducing smaller managers to large allocators.

FOR DUE DILIGENCE

Verify what was previously unverifiable.

Operational due diligence consultants face a structural problem: the most important claims to verify are the ones that cannot be verified without access to proprietary logic. Lintel resolves this by providing cryptographic proof of claim validity without exposing the underlying logic — the consultant completes the verification step, the manager retains the intellectual property. The result is completed due diligence reports with evidence rather than attestations, reduced liability exposure, and a faster cycle for all parties.
